The Curbside Crisis

Due to labor shortages and high contamination rates, St. Louis has officially pivoted to a "Drop-Off" model. Placing recyclables in your alley bin now almost guarantees they end up in a landfill. Our mission is to provide the maps and info needed to keep our community green.
