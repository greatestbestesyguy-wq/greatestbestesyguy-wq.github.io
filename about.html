The Mission

This website is the culmination of our Advocacy Project for Mr. Boyle’s class. Our goal is to address the critical breakdown of the recycling infrastructure in St. Louis. By synthesizing academic research, city policy, and direct interviews, we aim to provide a clear roadmap for residents to navigate the current "Drop-Off" model.

As per the project requirements, we have curated 15 high-quality academic sources and conducted a primary interview with a city authority to ensure our information is both accurate and actionable.
